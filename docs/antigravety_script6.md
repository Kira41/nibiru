# `script6.py`
**PowerMTA Analytics & Accounting Dashboard**

## Overview
A diagnostic and forensic dashboard for understanding failed and successful delivery routes immediately after an email blast.

## How it works
It parses heavy and complex PowerMTA "accounting" CSV logs downloaded from the external sending servers. By running through the logs, it categorizes events and builds a local analytics view that details bounce reasons, successful deliveries, recipient-domain analysis, and generates diagnostic advice for the operator.

## Role
Actionable intelligence based on SMTP server logs offloaded into actionable graphical data.
